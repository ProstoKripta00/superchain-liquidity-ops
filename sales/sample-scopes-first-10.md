What I would check:
- Latest 7d vs 30d public liquidity trend.
- Volume, fee output, and weak-market split.
- Source consistency across public references.
- Any visible concentration, drop-off, or attribution gap.
- Clear limitations so the team knows what the data can and cannot prove.

Deliverables:
- PDF decision memo.
- CSV evidence.
- Source links.
- 3-5 action notes.

- Payment: OP, USDT, or USDC.

Timeline:
- Delivered in 3-5 business days based on the latest 7d / 30d public liquidity data.
