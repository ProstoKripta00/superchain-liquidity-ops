# Superchain Liquidity Ops Methodology

This document defines the measurement model for the grant MVP.

## Core Metrics

### TVL

Total value locked in a tracked DEX pool, denominated in USD.

Production sources may include:

- DEX subgraphs
- DefiLlama adapters
- indexed pool reserves
- onchain token balances plus USD oracle/pricing data

### 7-Day TVL Change

Weighted TVL movement over the last 7 days.

Used to detect:

- growing pools
- liquidity outflows
- pools that need incentive review

### 30-Day Volume

Aggregate swap volume over the previous 30 days.

Used to evaluate whether liquidity is actually being used.

### 30-Day Fees

Fees generated by the pool over the previous 30 days.

This is the main measurable output for DEX revenue impact.

### Fee Efficiency

Fee efficiency compares fees generated against liquidity supplied:

```text
fee_efficiency = 30d_fees / TVL
```

This helps reviewers distinguish deep but underused liquidity from pools that generate meaningful activity.

### Liquidity Depth

Approximate depth available around active price ranges.

Production implementation will depend on pool type:

- concentrated liquidity pools: active tick/range analysis
- stable pools: reserves and slippage simulation
- volatile pools: reserve depth and route simulation

## Pool Health Labels

### Strong

Pool has healthy TVL, positive or stable TVL trend, reasonable fee output, and no obvious depth issue.

### Watch

Pool has mixed signals, such as deep TVL with weaker fees, rising utilization with shallow depth, or negative short-term TVL change.

### At Risk

Pool has low depth, declining TVL, weak fee output, or signs that incentives may not be producing measurable impact.

## Reviewer Outputs

The MVP should produce grant-review artifacts:

- CSV export by pool
- chain-level TVL and fee summaries
- watchlist of underperforming pools
- 7d/30d trend reports
- notes explaining why a pool is marked Strong, Watch, or At Risk

## Initial Live Data Scope

Phase 1 production adapters should cover:

- OP Mainnet
- Base
- selected priority DEX pools
- TVL, volume, fees, and basic health scoring

Later phases can add more Superchain networks, DEXs, alerting, APIs, and automated reviewer reports.
